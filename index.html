What?

Hello! Welcome to joss dot cat, a small website focused on cat facts that I enjoy thinking about. I also have links and references to other places to learn about cats. I’m still learning and there’s a lot I don’t know. I welcome comments and criticism.

Why?

Cats are the best and coolest animals currently alive today.1 I like thinking about them and looking at them and maybe you do, too.

  • 1 “Cat” refers to any animal in the Felidae family.
